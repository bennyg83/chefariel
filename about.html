Chef Ariel Fish

Graduate from the Jerusalem Culinary Institute, Chef Ariel has been working in professional kitchens in and out of Jerusalem for many years. Now, for the last couple of years, Chef Ariel commands his kitchen in Beit Shemesh, offering delectable dishes in many styles and cuisines.

  • Healthier Food - The focus is on healthy food that tastes amazing, while understanding our Jewish roots, and bringing classic dishes into the modern age.
  • All ingredients used by Chef Ariel are strictly Mehadrin - See more below




Driven By Passion

Dishes made with love and understanding of a healthy life

Chef Ariel Fish focuses on healthy cooking. Using better oils, while avoiding soybean and generic vegetable oils, cooking that makes you feel good. Even though we are in Israel, you should always expect the high standards of Chef Ariel Fish. Using the freshest available ingredients and high quality as well, Chef Ariel Fish cooks from the heart, with a home-style feeling to each dish. And Chef Ariel is always available for questions regarding menu items and dishes.

Experienced in cooking for allergies and preferences, Chef Ariel will prepare you menu according to your needs and dietary instructions. Just let the chef know and your wishes will be taken care of. Chef Ariel can also help you plan your menu to your specifications.





Chef Ariel is a food aficionado

Chef Ariel is always researching new dishes and preparation methods. He appreciates good food and good food talk. Not everyone in the kosher world understands that, therefore Chef Ariel can help you figure out the proper menus and food items for your party or Shabbos meals.

Coming up with menus weekly for Shabbos is difficult, so leave it up to the Chef! Check out the weekly specials on Chef Ariel's Facebook page.
